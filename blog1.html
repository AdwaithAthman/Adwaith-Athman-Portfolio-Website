JOURNEY TO THE GOAL

Motivation Success Failure Pursuing Dream

3 July 2021 | 3 min read


It is always good to dream big and follow one’s passion. The results are meant to be followed organically, depending upon the earnest efforts we put forth. But even after putting in a lot of effort, if still, the desired results are not coming along our way, what shall we do?


It’s a question that haunts most of us. In a country of a billion population, things get more obfuscated. The competition is getting stiffer and harder than ever and one would find it very difficult to pursue his dream. The obstacles one has to tackle are becoming more challenging and most would find themselves stuck in similar sorts of horrendous situations, not knowing what to do. Usually many would end up losing all hope and start blaming themselves or the system. But is this the right way? Is there a remedy to the problem?


Failure and Success are just part of every game, we could end up on any side . A mere failure does not mean the end of the line, it's just an indicator to show our efforts are not up to the mark. More sincere ,vigorous and determined approach is the need of the hour and would also help to turn the tide in our favour.


Patience is the other key attribute that could save us in those scenarios. Pressuring ourselves is not a solution, it could only ask for more troubles. How hard you pressure yourself would only add up the mental fatigue. Getting worried about the past chapters in life which cannot be overturned is just a waste of valuable time. Most people are nervous and tense about how their past actions would affect their future . By doing so they are forgetting they have a life to live in the present. In the process, a big chunk of time would be wasted which could have been utilized to make a difference in the future.


“Time isn’t precious at all, because it is an illusion. What you perceive as precious is not time but the one point that is out of time: the Now. That is precious indeed. The more you are focused on time — past and future — the more you miss the Now, the most precious thing there is.”

-Eckhart Tolle


When you think of your FUTURE GOAL, sometimes you start feeling overwhelmed. Have you ever encountered similar situations? It's something that I have experienced in my life.


In the run for achieving this big goal, we often forget to celebrate the small victories, they are overlooked. But what’s hilarious is that we have all the time in the world to get worried about even the smallest slump in the process. Actually there is no harm in getting slightly worried, anyway we have to correct these mistakes then only we can progress. But what generally happens in most cases is that people would overthink the situation and make it more convoluted to overcome.

These big goals are not something meant to be achieved overnight, they require lots of patience, hard work and perseverance. Instead of worrying about this impending tedious journey, it would be great if one is able to break down these big goals into mini-goals. It would help to take things, one at a time. On top of that, if we could allocate some time for celebrating these little accomplishments in our journey, it would help in our mental well-being. Celebrating these small successes can amplify one’s mood and productivity, alleviate depression, and help us to know our real worth. In this way, we can be more goal-oriented and build a habit of being more effective. It also helps us to enjoy the process rather than getting carried away fearing the tough journey at hand to realize our ambition.


"Celebrate the journey. It's not all about the destination. Savour all of your successes, even the small ones."

-Dawn Gluskin
